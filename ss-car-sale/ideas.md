# S&S Car Sale — Design Brainstorm

## Approach 1: Dark Automotive Prestige
**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalist Automotive / Dark Luxury
**Core Principles:** High contrast, dramatic car photography, bold typography, yellow-gold accents
**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) backgrounds with amber/gold (#f5a623) accents — evoking power, trust, and premium feel
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with asymmetric content blocks, alternating dark/light sections
**Signature Elements:** Bold uppercase headings, thick yellow horizontal rules, dark overlay cards
**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth scroll reveals, hover lift effects on cards
**Animation:** Fade-in-up on scroll, subtle parallax on hero
**Typography System:** Oswald (bold display) + Lato (body) — strong automotive feel

## Approach 3: Bold Street-Level Automotive
**Design Movement:** Urban Automotive / Industrial Bold
**Core Principles:** Raw energy, diagonal cuts, strong yellow-black contrast, motion-inspired
**Color Philosophy:** Jet black (#0d0d0d) with electric yellow (#f5c518) — high visibility, sporty, dynamic
**Layout Paradigm:** Diagonal section dividers, full-width image strips, overlapping elements
**Signature Elements:** Diagonal clip-paths between sections, speed-line decorative elements, bold stat counters
**Interaction Philosophy:** Fast, snappy transitions that mirror the energy of driving
**Animation:** Quick slide-ins, scale-up on hover, diagonal wipe transitions
**Typography System:** Barlow Condensed (headings) + Nunito Sans (body)

## Selected Approach: Dark Automotive Prestige (Approach 1)
Closely mirrors the reference site's dark/amber aesthetic while elevating it with modern design principles.
- Oswald for bold uppercase headings, Lato for readable body text
- Charcoal/dark backgrounds for hero, testimonial, CTA banner sections
- Amber/gold (#f5a623) for accents, buttons, and highlighted text
- White sections for content areas (about, features, services)
- Full-bleed hero with dramatic car photography overlay
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations
